Event snapshot

  • Theme: Data visualization & business intelligence.
  • Format: Screening round + final round with live dataset.
  • Participants: Teams of up to 3 or individual entries.
  • Tools: Microsoft Excel, Power BI, Tableau, Canva.
  • Device policy: Participants bring their own laptops.

Why sponsor Data Dash?

How it runs

  • A common dataset is shared for the screening round; teams submit a basic dashboard.
  • Shortlisted entries advance to the final, where a new dataset is given on the spot.
  • Each team presents its dashboard to the judges in 5–7 minutes.

Judging lens

  • Creativity & originality in visuals and approach.
  • Data storytelling and clarity of insights.
  • Technical soundness and appropriate use of tools.
  • Overall user experience of the dashboard.
